Classic Brazillian Petit Gateau

Ingredients

  • 250 grams of butter
  • 5 eggs
  • 5 egg yolks (in addition to the other eggs)
  • 170 grams of sugar
  • 300 grams of dark chocolate
  • 50 grams of icing sugar
  • 100 grams of wheat flour
  • 4 small round pans for the cake (around 6 centimeters)
  • Vanilla ice cream
