Classic Brazillian Petit Gateau

Ingredients

  • 250 grams of butter
  • 5 eggs
  • 5 egg yolks (in addition to the other eggs)
  • 170 grams of sugar
  • 300 grams of dark chocolate
  • 50 grams of icing sugar
  • 100 grams of wheat flour
  • 4 small round pans for the cake (around 6 centimeters)
  • Vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees celcius
  2. Grease the pans with butter and store
  3. Melt the butter and chocolate in the microwave
  4. By hand mix the eggs, the yolks the sugar and at last the flour
  5. Add the melted chocolate to the mix and continue
  6. Turn the dough into the pans and put in the oven for 4 minutes
  7. Unmold the cakes in a plate and ice them with icing sugar
  8. Serve immediately with ice cream
