Japanese Fried Rice

Description

A simple recipe to make during lazier days by using older white rice left in the fridge. Turning leftovers into a great delicious meal

While the finishing sauce may sound small at the end it is crucial for the final dish, so dont forget it okay?

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teacups of white rice
  • 1 grated carrot
  • 2 teaspoons of white sesame
  • 2 tablespoons of shoyu
  • Olive oil
  • Watercress

Steps

  1. Peel and grate the carrot
  2. In a small bowl break the eggs and stir them with a fork. Season them with salt as you wish
  3. Take a large frying pan to low heat and put one teaspoon of olive oil and the stirred eggs.
    Keep in low heat until the ends get firm for about three minutes.
  4. With a silicone spatula slowly fold the omlet onto itself until you form a little roll,
    Transfer it to another plate and wait.
  5. With the same pan increase to medium heat and put another teaspoon of olive oil with the carrots.
    Season with a pinch of salt and saute until wilted. Add a tablespoon of olive oil with the rice and sesame seeds.
    let them fry for about twelve minutes.
  6. When the rice grains are loose and dry add the shoyu and stir for one minute.

For the sauce

Ingredients

  • One tablespoon of honey
  • One tablespoon of rice vinagre
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon of shoyu

Preparation

In a pot with a lid add all the ingredients and shake until mixed.
