Japanese Fried Rice

Description

A simple recipe to make during lazier days by using older white rice left in the fridge. Turning leftovers into a great delicious meal

While the finishing sauce may sound small at the end it is crucial for the final dish, so dont forget it okay?

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teacups of white rice
  • 1 grated carrot
  • 2 teaspoons of white sesame
  • 2 tablespoons of shoyu
  • Olive oil
  • Watercress
